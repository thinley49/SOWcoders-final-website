About


Help is a fundraising website developed in october 2021 to help raise funds for local charity organizations in Bhutan such as The Bhutan Society Trust Fund , Bhutan cancer society, Bhutan kidney Foundation and many more who are dedicated to provide social, spiritual, and financial support to affected individuals.

Like many other non-profit organizations, the non-profit organizations in Bhutan also struggle with insufficient funding associated to increased demand for their services.

It is important that the organizations achieve financial sustainability in order to provide services to those affected with renewed interest and promt actions.

One of the ways to help the organizations ensure sustainable finance is through fundraising.


It would be of an immense help if you can donate any amount either directly to those mentioned websites or through this Help website and make life easier for those affected.
