About

Help is a fundraising website developed in october 2021 to raise funds for local charity organizations in Bhutan such as The Bhutan Society Trust Fund , Bhutan cancer society, Bhutan kidney Foundation who are dedicated to provide social,spiritual, and financial support to affected individuals


It would be of an immense help if you all can donate any amount either directly to those mentioned websites or through this Help website and make life easier for those affected.

Mission Statement

To insure the financial viability and stability of our local non-profit organizations. and to create awareness about the organizations.


The funds raised from this website will be used judiciously to fund local charity organization. Donation would highly ensure sustainable finance of the organizations.
